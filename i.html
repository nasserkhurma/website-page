About Didier Drogba

Didier Drogba is a retired Ivorian professional footballer, born on March 11, 1978, in Abidjan, Ivory Coast. Drogba is best known for his prolific goal-scoring record and leadership on the field. He had a successful career playing for clubs like Chelsea and Galatasaray.
